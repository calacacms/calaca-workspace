# Licensing program review checklist (internal)

Use this when reviewing **nonprofit** and **education** license applications. Final eligibility is defined in your legal terms; this document is operational guidance only — not legal advice.

## General (both programs)

- [ ] Contact email is reachable and matches the claimed organization where possible (work/school domain preferred).
- [ ] Requested use matches the program (nonprofit vs education) — redirect if wrong form.
- [ ] Production domains (if any) are stated clearly; no obvious reseller or agency-for-hire misuse.
- [ ] Applicant agreed to privacy / data use for review (if you collect personal data).
- [ ] Decision recorded: approve / deny / need more info, with date and reviewer.

**Common decline reasons (examples)**

- For-profit company or personal commercial project framed as nonprofit.
- Missing verifiable affiliation (no org email, no registration details when expected).
- Request for unlimited client sites under a single school or charity license (unless your policy allows it).
- Hosting or SaaS resale of CalacaCMS to third parties without a commercial agreement.

---

## Nonprofit / charitable organizations

### Prefer approve when

- [ ] Registered nonprofit / charity / public-benefit organization **or** clear equivalent in jurisdiction (mission-aligned, non-distribution constraint, public activity).
- [ ] Website or public materials align with stated mission.
- [ ] License scope matches offer (e.g. one production site, or as defined in your policy).

### Ask for more information when

- [ ] Only a generic Gmail/Outlook address — request org domain, board letter, or registration number.
- [ ] New or unregistered group — request proof of activity (event pages, statutory docs, partnership letters).

### Prefer deny or escalate when

- [ ] Political campaigning or lobbying org if your policy excludes them (if applicable).
- [ ] Primarily fundraising for private gain; unclear public benefit.
- [ ] Organization appears inactive or fraudulent.

---

## Education program (schools, higher ed, foundations, teachers, students)

### Prefer approve when

- [ ] **Institution**: recognized school, district, university, or educational foundation; request from official IT or faculty email or letterhead.
- [ ] **Teacher / faculty**: verifiable institutional email or HR/faculty confirmation for the course or department named.
- [ ] **Student**: valid student email, stated course or portfolio use, reasonable domain count per your policy.

### Ask for more information when

- [ ] Role is unclear (staff vs contractor vs parent).
- [ ] Domain is personal — clarify whether use is for accredited coursework or institutional site.

### Prefer deny or escalate when

- [ ] Commercial training business marketed as “school” without nonprofit/education status you support.
- [ ] Request covers many unrelated client domains under an “education” claim.
- [ ] Credential cannot be verified after one follow-up.

---

## After approval

- [ ] Issue license key or internal order record with **scope** (domains, expiry, tier).
- [ ] Send email with **terms** for that tier (non-transferable, no resale, renewal rules).
- [ ] Add note for support: program type, expiry, escalation path.

## After denial

- [ ] Send neutral, short explanation (avoid arguing); point to commercial licensing if appropriate.
- [ ] Do not retain unnecessary personal data (follow your retention policy).
